Peer Evaluations


Nick Johnson's Website

From what I was able to review the website has five different pages and a style sheet. The colors are a little bright and I would recommend using the contrast checker. The clips tab has an error when you click on it and does not link to a page.

Will Nguyen's Website

I am very pleased with Will's webpage and have gotten some ideas to try on mine. The way his home page is really stands out and makes me want to continue exploring it.

CRAP:
  • Contrast: The colors Will has chosen for his website are warm and inviting and overall the website has a very pleasent apperance.
  • Repetition: The nav bar stays in the same place as you navigate through the pages and the style and coding and consistent.
  • Alignment: The website alignment is good, however some pictures have differnet margins from everything else and when looking at the rooms one picture is way too big to see.
  • Proximity: Proximity is okay, as said above some of the pictures have weird margins and need to be resized. The paragraphs in the about us are small and could be spaced a little further apart.
