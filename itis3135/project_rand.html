Project Information

The purpose of the webpage will be to help the company provide information and background about themselves as well as allowing the customer to see reviews and leave a message about a possible event. Users who have weddings, parties and or events.The company provides a service of setting up events such as weddings, corporate events and social gatherings.

  • Client’s phone number (if they are willing to share) : private

Home page: The purpose of this page is to give a quick oversight of everything in other tabs in case someone just wants to browse quick. The page will help customers see what the company is about and some of the services they offer briefly. There will be hyperlinks to the about us and contact us pages so the customers can seamlessly learn more.

About Us: The purpose of the page is to allow customers to get some background information on the people they intend to hire. The targeted audience are the customers. The content of the page will likely have pictures of the two owners and a short paragraph about them. No data entry or verification will be needed. The only action that will happen on the page is when the user navigates to another tab.

Services: The purpose of the services tab will be to list and show what services are offered to customers. The targeted audience is once again the customers. The content of the page will be a list with a small description of each service. No data or verification needed. When the user clicks on the nav bar it will take them to another tab. A slide show of past events will be added to showcase the services.

Quote form: On this page there will be a form with a required of having your name, phone number and email down before submitting so you can be contacted. The page will be for both customers and the owners so they can get in contact with possible customers. There will need to be validations for the email and phone number. When submit or reset is pressed it clears the forms and the data is “sent” to the owners.

Where/ Contact information: On this page it will show the area of which the company operates along with contact information such as the phone number and email. This page will help the customers ask any one-off questions without having to fill out the quote form along with hours of operation. An imbedded maps showing Charlotte will be added. No data entry or verification will be needed.
