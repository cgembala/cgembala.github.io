Website Evaluations

Argren

Arngren is a website that sells from what I can tell atvs, scooters, and jeeps. The website is very chaotic and does not please visually. The sites products are all over and make it very hard to tell exactly what they are marketing. There is no menu that helps designate what page you are on or if there are other pages.

  • Contrast
  • Repetition
  • Alignment
  • Proximity

The site has bad contrast levels and it seems like they tried to get every color, there is also a lack of repetition becasue they change fonts so often. There is no alignment to be seen anywhere on this page and every element seems to be smashed together.

In Gold We Trust

In Gold We Trust is a website that sells gold jewlery and provides the user with a pleasent experience. The design almost seems vintage but it flows well and isn't over done. The links all load smoothly and I am able to tell where I am in the page. You are greeted with a welcome message and the option to sign up and save 10% on a purchase.

  • Contrast
  • Repetition
  • Alignment
  • Proximity

The website easily follows the contrast and repetition rules by giving the page a uniform font and pleasent colors. Alignment seems good on the element and they do not seem to over crowd the page.
